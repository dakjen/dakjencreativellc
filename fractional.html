What It Is

Running a company means your brand is usually the last thing that gets attention — and the first thing a potential client, partner, or investor notices. We step in so you don't have to choose.

DakJen Creative's Creative Consulting service is your dedicated brand department — overseeing campaigns, refining positioning, managing content strategy, producing collateral, and making sure every touchpoint is aligned, credible, and unmistakably you. The creative intelligence of an in-house team, without the overhead.

Who It's For

We work with founders, executives, and mission-driven organizations who think clearly, move fast, and need a creative partner who can keep up. Small business owners scaling past $500K, executives building a personal brand alongside their company, organizations doing important work that deserves to be seen clearly.

We also work with founders who are just getting started — if the work is sharp and the vision is clear. If you're building something that matters, we want to be part of it from day one.

What's Included

Every engagement is scoped to your needs. Typically this includes monthly brand strategy sessions, campaign oversight and creative direction, social media content planning and management, brand voice development and messaging refinement, collateral production — decks, one-pagers, digital assets — vendor and creative team coordination, and quarterly brand audits.

The Entry Point — Brand Audit

Not sure if a retainer is right yet? Start with a Brand Audit. We assess your brand, messaging, digital presence, and positioning — then deliver a written report with clear recommendations. It's the lowest-risk way to see how we think before committing to an ongoing engagement.
