Notable Amplify — Platform & Revenue Strategy

You have expertise, credibility, and an audience waiting. Let's build the platform that monetizes it.

Notable Amplify is for the founder who has done the work, built the reputation, and knows she has more to offer — but hasn't had the time, the strategy, or the right partner to package it all up and launch it. We sit down with you, assess what you're sitting on, and build a complete platform and revenue stream roadmap. Then we produce the deliverables you need to actually launch.

What We Identify & Build For You

  • Online course concept — outlined, structured, and ready to build
  • Book proposal — developed and ready to pitch or self-publish
  • Blog or newsletter strategy — topic framework, monetization plan, launch content
  • LinkedIn content strategy — 2 months of posts, written and ready to publish
  • Fireside chat or speaking series — topics, budget, run-of-show, promotional content
  • Podcast concept or guest strategy — format, positioning, outreach templates
  • New line of business — named, branded, priced, and launch-ready

The Ecosystem Advantage

Amplify clients get more than strategy and content. They get access to our curated network of execution partners — tech builders who can bring your course platform to life, publicists who can get you placed and seen, event planners who can execute your live experiences. We don't just tell you what to do. We introduce you to the people who help you do it.

LinkedIn Voice Intensive

Your expertise deserves a powerful, consistent online presence.

LinkedIn is often the first place a potential client, investor, or partner goes to check you out. If your profile doesn't reflect your expertise — or you're not posting consistently — you're leaving real opportunity on the table every single day.

The LinkedIn Voice Intensive is a focused engagement where we clarify exactly how you want to show up online, sharpen every element of your profile, and develop a content voice that's distinctly yours. You leave with a strategy you can use and the first two months of content ready to go.

What's Included

  • Full profile audit and rewrite — headline, about section, featured, experience framing
  • Brand voice and content pillar development
  • Content strategy and posting framework tailored to your goals
  • 2 months of LinkedIn posts — written, edited, and ready to publish
  • 30-day content calendar
  • Guidance on engagement strategy and growing your network intentionally

Who It's For
