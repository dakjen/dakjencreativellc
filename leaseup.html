What We Do

DJC Marketing runs the full lease-up marketing cycle for affordable housing developments across Washington D.C., Baltimore, Maryland, Virginia, and the greater DMV — from the moment you're ready to announce to the day you hit stabilization.

We are not a general marketing agency. We know how affordable housing is financed, what income-qualifying means, how lottery and waitlist processes work, and what regulators and investors need to see. That specificity is what makes our campaigns convert.

DJC Marketing is a Black-owned, woman-owned business currently in the process of obtaining MBE and WBE certification. We handle every piece of the marketing operation so your property management team can focus on operations — not outreach.

What's Included

01 — Pre-Leasing & Strategy
Build momentum before day one.

Before a single ad runs, we build your campaign strategy around your unit mix, income tiers, target applicant profile, and timeline. We create a pre-leasing plan that builds interest and a waitlist before doors open — so you launch with momentum, not from zero.

02 — Digital Campaign Management
Listings, paid ads, and organic — fully managed.

Apartment listings on Apartments.com, Zillow, Craigslist, and AffordableHousing.com. Targeted Facebook and Instagram paid ads. Google Display. Organic social. Every ad is geo-targeted, income-aware, and optimized weekly based on performance data.

03 — Traditional Media
Physical presence where it counts.

Billboards, construction-site banners, radio spots, newspaper ads, and community publication placements. We handle placement, production, and vendor coordination — you don't manage any of it.

04 — Community Outreach
Trust built on the ground, not just online.

Partnerships with local churches, community organizations, workforce agencies, and social services. We attend job fairs, host information sessions, and build the ground-level trust that turns awareness into completed applications.

05 — Applicant Communication
Every inquiry handled, nothing dropped.

We manage all inbound inquiry volume — answering questions, distributing applications, tracking submissions, and keeping prospective residents informed. Your property management team stays in the loop without being buried in calls.

Add-Ons

Application Support

Our application success manager personally contacts applicants whose submissions were initially rejected — helping them correct errors, gather missing documents, and resubmit. This keeps your pipeline full of qualified candidates who otherwise fall off. At 9410 Hough, this single add-on drove a greater than 20% re-apply rate.

Model Unit Staging & Photography

We design, source, deliver, stage, and photograph a tour-ready model unit from scratch — handling every vendor and every detail. Professional photography is included. After lease-up, furniture can be donated to an affiliated nonprofit and distributed to incoming residents, giving the owner a charitable contribution benefit while supporting the community.

How We Work

A structured approach that builds momentum and sustains it.

Phase 1 — Preparation & Pre-Launch

We spend the first month preparing listings, building creative assets, lining up media placements, and staging the model unit. We soft-launch to gauge early interest before the full push begins.

Phase 2 — Full-Scale Push & Follow-Up

Full campaign across all selected channels. Applicant support kicks in — tracking, follow-up outreach, document support. We monitor qualification rates and adjust targeting to maintain velocity through stabilization.
