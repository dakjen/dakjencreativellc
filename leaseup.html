What We Do

DJC Marketing is a results-driven lease-up marketing firm with 5 years of deep roots in housing development and community engagement. We understand how developments are financed, how timelines work, and exactly what's at stake when units sit empty. That knowledge — combined with a creative, high-impact approach — is what gets results traditional marketing agencies can't deliver.

We help affordable housing projects reach full occupancy by generating qualified leads, managing the applicant process, and maintaining clear communication between owners, property managers, and prospective residents. With on-the-ground outreach, targeted marketing, and proactive coordination, we keep leasing momentum strong and stakeholders aligned.

"DJC Marketing doesn't just market units — we move developments forward."

The Problem We Solve

Low application conversion rates. Slow leasing velocity. Limited property management staff capacity. Poor community awareness. Lack of transparency and reporting to stakeholders. Reputation and investor confidence at risk. These are the things we were built to fix.

Add-Ons

Application Support

A full re-review of initially rejected applications via our application success manager. We contact applicants, help them correct errors, and guide them through the process — maximizing your completed application pool and giving qualified candidates a fair shot. At 9410 Hough, this yielded a greater than 20% re-apply rate.

Model Unit Staging

We design, procure, deliver, stage, and decorate a tour-ready model unit — with professional photography included. We also offer a 50% donation perk: furniture can be donated to our affiliated nonprofit after lease-up and distributed to residents, giving the owner a charitable contribution benefit while supporting the community.

How We Work

A structured approach that builds momentum and sustains it.

Phase 1 — Preparation & Pre-Launch

We spend the first month preparing listings, building creative assets, lining up media placements, and staging the model unit. We soft-launch to gauge early interest before the full push begins.

Phase 2 — Full-Scale Push & Follow-Up

Full campaign across all selected channels. Applicant support kicks in — tracking, follow-up outreach, document support. We monitor qualification rates and adjust targeting to maintain velocity through stabilization.
