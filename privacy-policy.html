Privacy Policy

This policy explains how DakJen Creative LLC collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website or work with us.

Last updated: June 6, 2026

DakJen Creative LLC ("DakJen Creative," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you visit dakjencreative.com (the "Site"), contact us, or engage our services. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here.

In plain terms: this website does not use advertising trackers, analytics pixels, or first-party cookies, and it has no contact forms. We only receive personal information when you choose to email us or book a call, and we use a small number of third-party services (described below) to run the Site.

1. Who We Are

DakJen Creative LLC is the controller responsible for the personal information described in this policy. You can reach us at business@dakjencreative.com. We operate from New York, NY and Baltimore, MD (United States).

2. Information We Collect

Information you give us directly. The Site does not have contact or sign-up forms. You share information with us only when you choose to:

  • Email us (for example, at business@dakjencreative.com) — we receive your name, email address, and anything you include in your message.
  • Book a call through our scheduling link — you provide your name, email address, and any details you add when scheduling. This booking is handled by Google Calendar (see Section 4).

Information collected automatically. Like virtually all websites, when you visit the Site our hosting provider automatically records standard server-log data such as your IP address, browser type, device information, referring page, and the date and time of your visit. This is used for security and to keep the Site running. We do not run analytics software or advertising trackers on the Site.

3. Cookies

The Site does not set its own (first-party) cookies, and we do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies. The only third-party connections the Site makes are to Google Fonts and to our Google Calendar scheduling link (described below), which may set or read cookies under Google's control when those services load or are used. You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time without affecting the core content of the Site.

4. Third-Party Services We Use

We rely on a small number of reputable providers to operate the Site. Each processes data under its own privacy policy:

  • Website hosting — our host stores the Site and maintains server logs (including IP addresses) for delivery and security.
  • Google Fonts — the Site loads typefaces from Google's font servers. When a page loads, your browser's IP address and request information are sent to Google so the fonts can be delivered. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Calendar (scheduling) — our "Book a Call" link opens Google's appointment scheduling. If you book, the name, email, and details you enter are processed by Google on our behalf. See Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell or rent your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we receive to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you;
  • Schedule and conduct calls, meetings, and consultations;
  • Provide and manage our services to clients;
  • Operate, secure, and maintain the Site;
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our legal rights.

6. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK Visitors)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we process your personal information on the following legal bases: your consent (which you may withdraw at any time); our legitimate interests in operating and securing the Site and responding to inquiries; the performance of a contract when we work together; and compliance with legal obligations.

7. International Data Transfers

We are based in the United States, and our service providers (including Google and our host) may process data in the United States and other countries. If you access the Site from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S., where data-protection laws may differ from those in your country. Where required, our providers rely on recognized transfer mechanisms such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

8. Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described here — for example, to maintain our correspondence and business records and to meet legal obligations. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize it.

9. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information we hold. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of it; to object to or restrict certain processing; and to withdraw consent. To exercise any of these rights, email us at business@dakjencreative.com. We will respond as required by applicable law and will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

EEA/UK residents (GDPR). You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

California residents (CCPA/CPRA). You have the right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it, to request access or deletion, to correct inaccurate information, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under California law, and we do not use it for cross-context behavioral advertising. You may exercise your rights using the contact details above, and you may use an authorized agent to do so.

11. Third-Party Links

The Site links to other websites and platforms, including our affiliated brands (such as Go Be Notable, Elitewise Escapes, Badjr, The Wren Club, and Design Domain) and external services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites, and we encourage you to review their policies.

12. Children's Privacy

The Site is intended for a general, business audience and is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected on this page. Your continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, contact us at business@dakjencreative.com.
